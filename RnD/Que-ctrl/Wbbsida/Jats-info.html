About JATS

Introducing the Jenssen Asset Tracking Solution (JATS), a revolutionary approach to asset management that transforms the way businesses, such as hotels, track and manage their valuable items.
With JATS, we harness the power of RFID technology to seamlessly tag and monitor assets, ensuring that every item—from televisions to furniture—is accounted for and easily accessible.
Imagine a hotel where every asset is connected, providing staff with real-time visibility into the location and status of each item.
Our user-friendly web platform allows hotel management and staff to view, manage, and optimize their assets effortlessly.
With JATS, you can reduce loss, streamline operations, and enhance guest experiences by ensuring that everything is in its right place.
Join us in revolutionizing asset management with JATS, where efficiency meets innovation.
